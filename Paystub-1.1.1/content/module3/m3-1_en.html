Comparing Both Types of Pay Stub

Depending on the information you are looking for, you may need to refer to the Phoenix Pay Stub over the CWA Pay Stub, or vice-versa.

The next section will highlight how Overtime (Extra Duty Pay) and Leave without pay (LWOP) are displayed on both Pay Stubs.

Before we continue, let’s quickly recap …

  • So far, you have seen that essential information can be obtained on both pay stubs.
  • In Lesson 1, you learned that CWA Pay Stubs display the pay period in which overtime (OT) was worked or leave without pay (LWOP) was taken.
  • In Lesson 2, you learned that Phoenix Pay Stubs displays the portion of benefits the employer pays on your behalf.

Let’s have a closer look at some of the differences.

Only the CWA Pay Stub will confirm Only The Phoenix Pay Stub will confirm
❯ Your province of work ❯ Your mailing address
❯ The Pay Period during which OT was worked ❯ Your Personal Tax Exemptions
❯ The Pay Period during which the LWOP occurred ❯ Your additional Federal and/or Quebec Tax deducted (at your request)
❯ Which deductions are taxable vs non-taxable
❯ Your Employer Paid Benefits
