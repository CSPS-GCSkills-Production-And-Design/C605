A few words on mandatory taxes:

If you work in the Province of Quebec, you are also required to pay the Quebec Parental Insurance Plan (PPIP), and Quebec Pension Plan (QPP) deductions will be withheld instead of CPP.

CIT

Federal (Canada) Income Tax. For all provinces besides Quebec, Federal and Provincial income tax is deducted as a single amount.
