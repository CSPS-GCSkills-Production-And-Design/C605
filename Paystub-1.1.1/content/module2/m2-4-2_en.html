The recovery of Leave Without Pay will appear in the Earnings section of your Phoenix Pay Stub.

If a period of Leave Without Pay (LWOP) of 5 consecutive days or less:

has been approved in Phoenix, it will appear twice in the Earnings Section:

  • once as LWOP (this line only confirms the LWOP has taken place and appears for Pension calculation purposes)
  • once as a negative amount which is deducted from your pay.

The Phoenix Pay Stub will confirm:

  • the type of LWOP
  • the number of hours recovered

The Phoenix Pay Stub will not confirm:

  • the dates that correspond to the LWOP

Note: The same process would apply to the recovery of statutory holidays for part-time employees.
