Phoenix Pay Stub

Earnings - Overtime

Payments that include Overtime (also referred to as Extra Duty Pay) will include any Overtime approved in Phoenix by your manager. Paid Overtime will appear in the Earnings section of your Phoenix Pay Stub.

The Phoenix Pay Stub confirms:

  • the Type of Overtime
  • the Overtime Code
  • the Number of Hours paid
  • the Rate
  • the Amount paid

Note: If Overtime hours were worked in different Pay Periods, the hours would be combined by Type of Overtime.

How Can You Calculate Your OT Hourly Rate?

If you are a full-time employee working 37.5 hours per week, a quick way to calculate your Basic Pay (or Acting Pay) hourly rate is to divide your annual salary by 1956.6 hours:

$72,905 ÷ 1956.6 = $37.26

Then, multiply this hourly rate by the multiplier (1.5, 1.75, 2.0, etc.) to obtain the OT hourly rate:

$37.26 × 1.5 = $55.89
$37.26 × 2.0 = $74.52

To learn more on how to calculate your hourly rate, please refer to Annex 1, which is available in the Resources section. To access Annex 1, simply select the Toolbox icon, then select Resources.
