Phoenix Pay Stub

Where Can I Find It?

To find out how to access your Phoenix Pay Stub, just follow the steps below.

Remember you must be connected to the Government of Canada Network to access your pay stub.

To access your Phoenix Pay Stub:

  1. Connect to the Government of Canada network at the following address.
  2. This will bring you to the Compensation Web Applications.
  3. From there, click on the Phoenix Pay System hyperlink to view your Phoenix Pay Stub.

You might be interested to know your Pay Stub will be available 2 days before the pay date.

Once in your Phoenix account:

  1. Click on the Main Menu.
  2. Click on Self Service.
  3. Click on Payroll and Compensation.
  4. Click on View Paycheck.
  5. From the Paycheque Selection, select the pay stub you want to look at.

Please note that the options in your drop down boxes will differ depending on your type of Phoenix Access.
