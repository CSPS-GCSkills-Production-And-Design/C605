Phoenix Pay Stub

Earnings Section

Earnings section of the Phoenix Pay Stub details your gross pay.

In this section, you will find things such as your:

  • Basic Pay (or Acting Pay)
  • Extra Duty Pay (Overtime)
  • Allowances (Bilingual Bonus, Pay in Lieu of Statutory Holidays, Vacation Pay, Education, Isolated Post, etc.)
  • Deductions for Leave Without Pay (LWOP) of 5 days or less, etc.

Did you know?

  • In Phoenix, the dates for which the amounts are paid or recovered will not be displayed.
  • Consequently, the Pay Periods in which the Overtime was worked or the LWOP took place will not be identified.
  • However, as seen in Lesson 1, these would be displayed on your Phoenix Pay Stub.
  • This can be useful if, for example, you worked many periods of overtime, and wish to confirm which ones are being paid on your current paycheque, and which ones have yet to be paid.
  • The Phoenix Pay Stub provides some useful information that is not available on the Phoenix Pay Stub, such as the Tax Data Section shown on the previous page.

Bilingual Bonus Allowance

The bilingualism bonus is an annual payment of $800, paid on a bi-weekly basis to eligible employees, occupying a bilingual position.

Information on the Bilingual Bonus Allowance?

Many criteria affect eligibility, such as valid Second Language Evaluation (SLE) results confirming the language requirements of the position are met.

Your employment status also could also affect your eligibility.

For more information on this allowance, please refer to the Bilingual Bonus Directive.
