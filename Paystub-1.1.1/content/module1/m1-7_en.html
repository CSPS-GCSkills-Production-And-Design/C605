CWA Pay Stub

Entitlements and Deductions Section

The Entitlements and Deductions section of the CWA Pay Stub details your gross pay. In this section, you will find things such as your:

  • Basic Pay (or Acting Pay)
  • Extra Duty Pay (Overtime)
  • Allowances (Bilingual Bonus, Pay in Lieu of Statutory Holidays, Vacation Pay, Education, Isolated Post, etc.)
  • Deductions for LWOP of 5 days or less, etc.

In addition, this section will confirm the dates for which the amounts are paid or recovered.

Did you know?

  • Pay Periods start on a Thursday and end on the Wednesday 2 weeks later. Pay day is every other Wednesday.
  • There is a delay of 2 weeks between the last day of the Pay Period and the date at which you will receive your pay.
  • This means that you get paid for the work that concluded on the Wednesday 2 weeks prior to the pay day.

Note that, if your start day is Monday and the Pay Period ends on the Wednesday 2 days later, your first pay would only be for 3 days.
