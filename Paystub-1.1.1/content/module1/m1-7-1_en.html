Entitlements and Deductions - Overtime

Payments that include Overtime (also referred to as Extra Duty Pay) will include any Overtime approved in Phoenix by your manager. Paid Overtime will appear in the Entitlements and Deductions section of your CWA Pay Stub.

The CWA Pay Stub confirms:

  • the Pay Period in which the Overtime was worked
  • the Type of Overtime
  • the Number of Hours paid
  • the Rate
  • the Amount paid

Note: If Overtime hours were worked in different Pay Periods, the amounts would be broken down by Pay Period.
