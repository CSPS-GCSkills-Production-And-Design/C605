This line displays taxable employer-paid benefits. These are not deducted from your pay. Please see the note below.

Taxable Allowance Benefits

What are Taxable Allowance Benefits?

These are employer-paid benefits which are not deducted from your pay.

They are, however, reported as part of your income for income tax purposes.

If you work in the province of Quebec, the taxable benefits amount will not appear in this section, but rather under the “Entitlements and Deductions” section.
