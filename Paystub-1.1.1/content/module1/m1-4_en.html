CWA Pay Stub

Statutory Deductions Section

A few words on the statutory deductions:

The Statutory Deductions section of the CWA Pay stub details the incometax, Canada Pension Plan (CPP) contributions, and Employment Insurance(EI) premiums your employer must deduct from your pay.

Do you work in the Province of Quebec? If so, you are also required topay the Quebec Parental Insurance Plan (PPIP), and the Quebec Pension Plan (QPP) will be withheld instead of CPP.

Finally, depending on your employment status, you may also be required to contribute to the Public Service Pension Plan.

What is the Year to Date column?

Year to Date amounts are the total amounts you have paid so far for the current tax year for each type of deduction, or received for each type of entitlement.
