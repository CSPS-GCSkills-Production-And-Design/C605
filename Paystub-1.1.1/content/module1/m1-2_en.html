CWA Pay Stub

General Information Section

The first section of your CWA Pay Stub will provide general information on your payment.

General section of the CWA Pay Stub
◀ Dated 2018/12/12 ▶
Name: TREMBLAY, J
Employee ID: 12345678
Business Unit: CSD00
Department: 624001
Province of Work: QC
Paycheque Number: 88888888
Financial Insitution and Account No.: *****************************0050

Continue below to find a detailed breakdown of the main sections of the pay stub.

Dated

The date on which the payment was issued (pay date). In CWA, you can select the blue arrows appearing before and after the date to view the pay stub dated before or after this date

◀ Dated 2018/12/12 ▶

Employee ID

This is your unique 9-digit personal record identifier (PRI). It is assigned to you for HR and Payroll purposes. Note that the leading "0"of your PRI does not appear in this field.

Employee ID:
012345678

Business Unit

For example, here CSD is the Department Code for ESDC in the pay system only.

Business Unit:
CSD00

Department

The 4 last digits identify your pay list.

Department:
624001

Province of Work

Your province of work on file. Your province of work is what determines the taxes which will be deducted from your pay, as well as which benefits are taxable.

Province of Work:
QC

Financial Institution and Account No

The last 4 digits of your bank account number for direct deposit.

Financial Institution and Account No.:
*****************************0050
