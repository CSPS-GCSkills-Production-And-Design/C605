CWA Pay Stub

Objectives

Upon completion of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Access your CWA (Compensation Web Applications) Pay Stub
  • Identify the earnings paid
  • Identify the taxes deducted
  • Identify the entitlements and deductions

This course developed by Employment and Social Development Canada (EDSC) is now available to all public servants through the Canada School of Public Service.

Be advised that authoritative documents, such as Collective Agreements and the Terms and Conditions of Employment, take precedence over the statements in this course.
